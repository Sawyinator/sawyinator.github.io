  • cmd Shutdown Timer
    A project i made that will give you a command prompt command that will set a shutdown timer for your pc. You can already manually do this, but with this itll automatically convert your hours, minutes and seconds into just seconds (what is required for the command to work) and then you can just paste that into cmd and press enter. Pretty simple idea, but it was mainly cause: A- It annoyed me opening up an hours to seconds converter and adding on minutes and whatnot and then typing out the command (if i remembered it) and then B- It was a project to help me learn more HTML, so hopefully i wont have to rely on vibecoding so much in future.
